About Me

Hey there, I am currently doing my PhD at Technical University of Denmark (DTU) in the Cryptography Group. My research is about Multi Party Computation and I mainly work on building new protocols. You can find a layman description of my project here.

I started my academic journey with a Bachelor's in Computer Science, focusing on media and design, at RheinMain University of Applied Sciences. Then, I moved to Darmstadt University of Applied Sciences to specialize in Cybersecurity. During my Master thesis, I worked on homomorphic transciphering for multi-factor biometric authentication. During my studies I've worked part time as a software developer.

Feel free to contact me via chizo@dtu.dk
